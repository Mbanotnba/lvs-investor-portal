LOLA VISION SYSTEMS INC. KONIKU INC.

LETTER OF INTENT

Non-Binding Expression of Commercial Intent

January 28, 2026

Between:

Koniku Inc. ("Koniku"), a California C Corporation

and

Lola Vision Systems, Inc. ("LVS"), a Delaware C Corporation

Purpose

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") expresses Koniku's intent to purchase LVS-250 Neural Compute Engines from Lola Vision Systems for integration into Koniku's sensor platform products, subject to the successful completion of technical qualification and execution of a definitive purchase agreement.

Volume Commitment

Product: LVS-250 Neural Compute Engine

Initial Volume (2027): Minimum 10,000 units

Target Pricing: $1,650 per unit (subject to final negotiation)

Estimated Value: $16,500,000 USD minimum

Timeline

The parties intend to complete technical qualification by Q3 2026 and execute a definitive supply agreement with binding purchase orders by Q4 2026, with initial production deliveries beginning Q1 2027.

Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality regarding the specific terms of this LOI and any technical or commercial information exchanged, except as required for financing, regulatory, or legal purposes.

Non-Binding Nature

This LOI represents a statement of intent and is not a binding commitment to purchase. A binding obligation will only arise upon execution of a definitive supply agreement containing mutually agreed terms and conditions.
